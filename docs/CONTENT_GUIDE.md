# Course content guide

Each published language pack should include:

- Language and regional variant, script, direction, and voice locale.
- CEFR level and measurable outcome per unit.
- Original or licensed text, audio, image, and video.
- Meaning, acceptable answers, distractors, hints, transliteration, and formality.
- Grammar note, culture note, safety review, and source/rights record.
- Review schedule tags and skill weights.
- Reviewer names and versions: author, linguist, native reviewer, QA reviewer.

## Quality gate

1. Linguist authors the learning outcome and progression.
2. Native speaker checks naturalness, region, and tone.
3. Second reviewer checks translation and acceptable variants.
4. Audio is checked against the approved script.
5. Automated schema, duplicate, empty-answer, and direction tests pass.
6. A release candidate is tested with target learners before publishing.

The bundled lexicon is starter content for demonstrating the engine. Do not market every generated node as a complete expert-authored course until language-specific packs pass this gate.
